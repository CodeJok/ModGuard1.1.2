What is ModGuard?
ModGuard is a Discord bot for Moderation and Server Protection.
How do I set up ModGuard?
Setting up ModGuard is simple. Follow our wizard guides for an easy setup.
Is ModGuard free?
ModGuard offers both free and premium plans with varying features.
Which platforms does ModGuard support?
ModGuard supports all platforms via Discord commands.
Can I customize filters?
Yes, ModGuard allows customization of word, link, and spam filters.
Does ModGuard offer analytics?
Currently, ModGuard does not offer analytics but may in the future.
How secure is ModGuard?
ModGuard is highly secure with robust protection mechanisms.
Can I integrate ModGuard with other tools?
ModGuard does not support third-party integrations at the moment.
What kind of support does ModGuard offer?
ModGuard offers 24-hour hosting and support for users worldwide.
Is ModGuard suitable for large communities?
Yes, ModGuard is suitable for communities of all sizes.
Does ModGuard support multilingual moderation?
Multilingual moderation is supported to some extent.
Who developed ModGuard?
ModGuard was developed by Frosty, Plex_men, and A-train.
